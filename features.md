## Cross-Cutting Feature Themes

### Table-Stakes Features

- Online booking engine for tours and activities
- Channel management and OTA distribution (TripAdvisor, GetYourGuide, Airbnb Experiences)
- Multi-day/multi-destination itinerary management
- Real-time availability and inventory management
- Payment processing and deposit collection
- Customer communication and confirmations
- Reporting on bookings and revenue
- Supplier/guide management
- Mobile-friendly booking interface

### Differentiating Features

- AI itinerary generation from client brief
- Dynamic itinerary adaptation (real-time weather/disruption monitoring)
- Guest personalisation and preference learning
- Guide assignment and logistics optimisation
- Integrated website builder (TrekkSoft, Lodgify-style)
- Premium itinerary presentation (Tourwriter, Vamoos)
- GDS integration for airline packaging (IATA-connected operators)
- Sustainability certification and carbon tracking
- AI-powered content creation and descriptions

### Underserved Areas

- Fully automated itinerary assembly from conversational AI
- Real-time operational anomaly detection and alerts
- Predictive guide performance and guest satisfaction
- Supplier relationship intelligence and performance scoring
- Regulatory compliance automation (IATA, EU Package Travel Directive)
- Dynamic pricing based on demand and operational constraints
- Autonomous guest communication and upselling

### AI-Augmentation Candidates

- Itinerary generation from client briefs (destination, budget, interests)
- Dynamic adaptation to real-time disruptions (weather, transport, venue closures)
- Guide assignment optimisation (language, expertise, availability)
- Guest personalisation and preference learning
- Supplier performance prediction and relationship scoring

## Recommended Feature Scope

**Must-have (MVP)**:
- Online booking engine for tours/activities with payment processing
- Channel management (OTA distribution to Airbnb, GetYourGuide, TripAdvisor)
- Multi-day itinerary builder and management
- Real-time availability and inventory sync
- Customer communication and confirmations
- Basic reporting (bookings, revenue, occupancy)
- Guide and supplier management

**Should-have (v1.1)**:
- AI itinerary generation from client briefs
- Dynamic pricing recommendations
- Guest personalisation and preference learning
- Integrated website builder
- Real-time disruption monitoring and alerts
- Supplier performance scoring
- GDS integration for airline packaging
- Sustainability tracking and carbon reporting

**Nice-to-have (backlog)**:
- Conversational AI booking assistant
- Guide assignment and logistics optimisation
- Predictive guest satisfaction and churn
- Automated regulatory compliance (IATA, EU Package Travel Directive)
- Dynamic itinerary adaptation (weather, transport)
- Advanced analytics and revenue optimisation
- Guest app with real-time itinerary updates
